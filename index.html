About The Band

Emerging from the shadows, Small Spaces blends the raw energy of hip-hop with the infectious hooks of pop and the lyrical depth of rap. Their sound is a haunting journey through atmospheric beats and introspective storytelling.

The band was formed in the quiet corners of the city, finding a unique voice in the spaces between noise and silence. This duality is the core of their identity, creating music that is both intimate and immense, personal yet universal. Prepare to get lost in the soundscape.
